Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) is a technique that combines a retrieval system with
a language model. Instead of relying only on what the model memorized during training, RAG
first fetches relevant chunks of text from an external knowledge base, then feeds those
chunks to the language model as context so it can generate a grounded, up-to-date answer.
